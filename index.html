Online Class During Pandemic Covid-19

Hi! I'm Naomi from 11 - 5, and I want to share about my experiences, the things I felt and the changes I felt during this pandemic.

To reduce the spread of the Corona virus, the government calls on all its people to carry out activities from home, such as working, studying, shopping, and so on. Then carry out the learning process from this house. There are positives and negatives that I personally feel. The positive side is, I have more free time than usual, then I can learn or do new things that I could not do before. But the downside is, I feel stressed, tired, and feel nausea after a few months at home. Sometimes I find it difficult to understand a given material and get bored quickly during study hours. Also my eyes often get sore from being in front of the screen too long.
