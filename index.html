Online Class During Pandemic Covid-19

Hi, Nice to meet you! I'm Naomi from 11 - 5. Here I want to share about things and the changes I find during this pandemic.

Because of the Covid-19 pandemic, many people have to carry out their activities from home. This was done to reduce the spread of the coronavirus, including students and teachers. Therefore a distance learning system or online school was created, where teaching and learning activities are carried out online.

In fact, I kind of can't believe that it's been almost a year and a half since this online school started. The funny thing is, there are things that I find amazing when I used to do this before a pandemic, and that was unusual during a pandemic. And one of them is getting up early. For Muslim friends, maybe waking up early is a common thing, considering they have to pray at dawn. However, for me personally, this is a surprising thing! "Wow, it turns out I could wake up that early."

Doing distance learning is arguably an exhausting thing. Because there is a lot to deal with in the process, such as unstable networks, declining economic conditions, less conducive learning environment, decreased learning motivation, etc. Then, due to being at home too long, I became more stressed and tired than before and I also became more sensitive than before. So, to forget about stress and other negative feelings, I spend a lot of my time sleeping or watching dramas, comedy skits, or playing games. Like a coin, everything must have 2 different sides. Although this online school period has many disadvantages, online school also has its own advantages such as a less busy schedule and increased rest time. During this online school period, I also became able to do things that I could not or rarely did before. For example, I like completing a novel, doing some me-time, and learning other languages such as Korean.

So, overall I am grateful for all kinds of strengths and weaknesses that I experienced because I believe through it all I was "matured." But still, I hope this pandemic can end quickly so everyone can carry out their normal activities. And honestly, I'm a little tormented because I can't hug or hold my friends' friends :(
